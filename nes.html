About the Founder

Aaron Richards, M.S., Founder & CEO

Aaron Richards, M.S., is a builder--of teams, systems, and standards that put families first. He grew up in a home led by two pastors, Dr. Kim and Dr. Osborne Richards, where faith, responsibility, and service weren't slogans; they were Tuesday. Aaron's north star is simple: create the kind of stability that let his wife, Brooke, be present with their two kids and support his parents through long-term care. Before launching Kingsley, he led ATN, a national education and community platform, where he learned how to turn complex ideas into clear, practical plans--and how much advisors can do when red tape gets out of the way.

Kingsley Wealth Partners is the culmination of that journey: a well-capitalized, advisor-first RIA designed for low bureaucracy, clear incentives, and real support. Advisors keep ownership of their client relationships, get founder-level mentorship, and plug into modern marketing and operating systems so they can spend more time serving people. As a minority-owned firm headquartered in Phoenix, Kingsley blends rigor with heart: straightforward planning, fiduciary duty once approved, and a culture that values long-term relationships over short-term noise.

Aaron's story reflects his personal background and values. Kingsley Wealth Partners will offer advisory services upon regulatory approval.
